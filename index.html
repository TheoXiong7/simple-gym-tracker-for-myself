Wednesday Tweak

Add +100–150 kcal of carbs on soccer day — extra rice pouch, banana, or oats — so you have fuel to play. Soccer also burns 500–800+ kcal, so if you start dropping faster than ~1.25 lb/wk or feel gassed, the fix is eat more, don't train more.

Track & Adjust

Weigh 3–4 mornings/week, same conditions, average the week.

The 2,100 is an estimate. After 2 weeks, check the weekly average against these rules. Protein stays fixed — only ever move carbs and fat.

→
Add ~150 kcal back
Sleep 7–9h

With 6 training days, recovery is what lets you keep the muscle and keep showing up. It's not optional.

Judge the average

Week 1 drops more (water) and the scale bounces daily. Trust the weekly trend and how you look & perform — not single readings.
